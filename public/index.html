Welcome to Hartland Country House

Nestled in the heart of Oudtshoorn, Hartland is a breathtaking French Country House that epitomizes elegance and tranquility. Set on an expansive estate, this extraordinary sanctuary offers an escape like no other. Whether bathed in the golden sun of summer, with a sparkling 15-meter swimming pool inviting you to relax and unwind, or during the cozy, crisp winter months, where an inviting indoor fireplace cast a warm glow, Hartland promises an experience that enchants year-round.

A home away from Home

Boasting 5 exquisitely appointed bedrooms, including 4 with luxurious ensuites, and an additional bedroom with a bathroom just across the hall, this home effortlessly combines comfort and sophistication. The interiors are adorned with tasteful décor, while the fully equipped kitchen and spacious living room create a welcoming atmosphere for both intimate gatherings and grand celebrations. Step outside onto the stunning stoep, where panoramic views of the pool and sprawling estate are framed by the lush beauty of ancient olive trees, offering the perfect backdrop for al fresco dining or quiet reflection. The grounds are a wonder in themselves, with abundant picnic spots for unforgettable family moments and a delightful jungle gym to keep the little ones entertained. Hartland is more than just a home—it’s a captivating retreat, where nature, luxury, and comfort come together in perfect harmony. Every detail of this magical property invites you to relax, rejuvenate, and experience the very best of countryside living.

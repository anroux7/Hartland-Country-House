Our Story

Tucked away in the heart of Oudtshoorn, Hartland is a hidden gem that embodies timeless elegance and a serene sense of tranquility. Inspired by the charm of a French Country House, this breathtaking estate was created as a sanctuary where luxury meets comfort.

Whether basking in the golden glow of summer by the shimmering 15-meter pool or embracing the warmth of a crackling fireplace in winter, every season at Hartland offers a unique and unforgettable experience.

From the moment you step onto our estate, we want you to feel the love and care that went into making this space truly special. Our vision was simple: to create a retreat where nature, comfort, and personal connection come together seamlessly.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

At Hartland Country House, our mission is to create a home away from home, where every guest feels welcomed, relaxed, and inspired. We strive to provide an unforgettable stay by blending comfort, hospitality, and unique local experiences.

Our Values

  • Authentic Hospitality – We treat every guest like family, ensuring warmth, care, and personalized service.
  • Comfort & Serenity – Every detail, from plush bedding to peaceful surroundings, is designed for your ultimate relaxation.
  • Attention to Detail – From the moment you arrive to the smallest touches in your room, we ensure every aspect of your stay is special.

Relax & Indulge Your Senses

Escape the ordinary and immerse yourself in an experience that awakens your senses. At Hartland Country House, we believe true relaxation is more than just a comfortable bed—it’s about the sights, sounds, scents, flavors, and textures that make your stay unforgettable. Let your senses guide you into a world of comfort and tranquility.

Breathtaking Views & Elegant Spaces

Wake up to golden sunrises or lush garden landscapes. Every corner of our space is thoughtfully designed with stylish interiors, warm lighting, and artistic touches that create a serene and inviting atmosphere. Whether you’re gazing at nature’s beauty or enjoying the cozy ambiance of our beautifully decorated rooms, every view is a feast for the eyes.

Serenity & Nature’s Melody

Let the soothing sounds of nature lull you into a state of peace. The gentle rustling of trees, the distant songs of birds greeting the morning, our environment is a sanctuary of calm. Even in the quiet of the night, you’ll enjoy restful sleep, undisturbed by city noise, allowing you to wake up refreshed and recharged.

Fresh, Inviting Aromas

Breathe in the crisp morning air, infused with the scent of blooming flowers and fresh linens. Wake up to the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee or herbal tea, setting the perfect tone for a slow, relaxing morning. Make every moment of your stay feel fresh and rejuvenating.

Let your senses come alive—stay with us and experience a world designed for pure comfort and indulgence.

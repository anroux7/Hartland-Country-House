← Self-catering Units

Hartland also offers two fully equipped self-catering units, giving you the flexibility to enjoy an independent and private stay while still being surrounded by the beauty of the property. Each unit is thoughtfully designed with all the amenities you need for a comfortable and relaxing experience, allowing you to prepare your own meals and truly make yourself at home.

These self-catering units provide the perfect balance of convenience and tranquility, making them an excellent option for those seeking a more private or extended stay while still enjoying all the serene features Hartland has to offer.

See all available room options below:

Garden Suite

Click on any of the images to enlarge

R2190 per night - Room only rate
R2490 per night - Bed & breakfast rate

The Hartland Garden Suite offers air-conditioned accommodation with everything you need for a peaceful and luxurious stay. The suite features a beautifully appointed bedroom and bathroom, with plush bed linen and towels to ensure your comfort. A flat-screen TV with streaming services, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchenette make it easy to settle in and feel at home. Step outside onto the terrace, where you can take in breathtaking mountain views while enjoying the peaceful ambiance of the garden. The room is enhanced with a carpeted floor, a cozy fireplace, and a serene vibe that invites you to unwind. The property also includes an outdoor dining area, ideal for meals surrounded by nature.

For your convenience, a continental breakfast featuring fresh pastries, fruits, and juice is available. If you prefer to cook at your own pace, the suite also offers a barbecue area for those leisurely outdoor meals. Whether you're enjoying the private pool, soaking in the mountain views, or simply relaxing in the suite, Hartland Garden Suite offers a serene and unforgettable retreat.

Loft

Click on any of the images to enlarge

R1890 per night - Room only rate
R2190 per night - Bed & breakfast rate

The loft suite is thoughtfully designed with a fully equipped kitchenette, allowing you to prepare your own meals with ease. The space is beautifully lit with soft, low lighting to create a cozy and inviting ambiance throughout. The suite features a comfortable living room area, complete with a TV and streaming services for your entertainment, as well as a dining room table where you can enjoy meals or spend quality time. The en-suite bathroom is elegantly designed with a shower, offering a relaxing and peaceful space to unwind.

For families or groups, the upstairs section can accommodate two teenagers, making it an ideal choice for those traveling with young adults. Please note that the stairs leading to the upstairs section are quite steep, so it’s best suited for those comfortable with a bit of extra elevation.

Additionally, the suite includes a private outdoor area with a table and chairs, where you can enjoy the beauty of nature while taking in a stunning sunset. This peaceful space provides the perfect setting to relax, reflect, and soak in the serene surroundings.

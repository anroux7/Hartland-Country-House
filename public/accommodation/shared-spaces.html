← Shared Spaces

Hartland’s shared spaces are designed for both relaxation and connection, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere for every guest. The spacious living room, adorned with elegant décor and plush seating, is perfect for unwinding with a book or gathering for meaningful conversations. Step onto the scenic stoep, where breathtaking views of the pool and sprawling estate create the ideal backdrop for morning coffee or sunset drinks. Whether sharing a meal at the outdoor dining area, enjoying the cozy ambiance of the indoor fireplace, or simply soaking in the tranquil surroundings, every corner of Hartland is crafted to bring people together in comfort and style.

Accommodation

This exquisite home features five beautifully appointed bedrooms, including four with private ensuites and one with a bathroom just across the hall, seamlessly blending comfort and sophistication. Stylish interiors, a fully equipped kitchen, and a spacious living area create a warm, inviting atmosphere for both intimate moments and lively gatherings. Outside, the stunning stoep offers breathtaking views of the pool and estate, framed by ancient olive trees—perfect for al fresco dining or quiet reflection. The expansive grounds boast charming picnic spots and a jungle gym for little ones, making Hartland more than just a stay—it’s a retreat where nature, luxury, and tranquility come together.
